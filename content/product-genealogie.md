*Product‑genealogie (productiegeschiedenis)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en traceerbaarheid & productidentificatie.

## Definitie

**Product-genealogie (productiegeschiedenis)** is de complete, gedocumenteerde geschiedenis van een product vanaf grondstoffen tot eindproduct, inclusief alle gebruikte materialen, bewerkingsprocessen, kwaliteitscontroles en betrokken personen. Het biedt volledige [[traceerbaarheid-en-productidentificatie|traceability]] voor kwaliteitsborging, compliance en probleemopsporing.

## Kenmerken

- **Complete material history**: [[heat-number|Heat numbers]], lot tracking, supplier information
- **Process documentation**: Alle bewerkingsstappen, machine parameters, operator records
- **Quality records**: Inspection results, [[statistical-process-control-software|SPC]] data, [[non-conformance-report|NCR]] history  
- **Configuration tracking**: [[engineering-change-order|ECO]] implementation, revision levels
- **Environmental conditions**: Temperature, humidity, atmospheric conditions tijdens productie
- **Time stamps**: Exacte tijden van alle bewerkings- en controlemomenten
- **Personnel tracking**: Operator certifications, welding qualifications
- **Supplier chain**: Complete upstream traceability naar raw materials

## Toepassing

**Metaalproduct genealogie:**
- **[[serienummer|Serial number]] tracking**: Individual product identification en history
- **[[batchnummer|Batch]] genealogie**: Material lot history voor bulk products
- **Weld genealogy**: Complete welding history met [[welding-procedure-specification|WPS]], welder qualification
- **Heat treatment tracking**: Furnace cycles, temperature profiles, certification

**Aerospace/Defense traceability:**
- **Critical components**: Complete material pedigree voor safety-critical parts
- **Certification tracking**: Compliance met military en aerospace standards
- **Life cycle management**: Service history, inspection records, overhaul documentation
- **Supply chain verification**: Counterfeit prevention door material authentication

**Automotive genealogy:**
- **[[production-part-approval-process|PPAP]] records**: Complete approval documentation history
- **Recall management**: Rapid identification van affected products
- **Warranty analysis**: Failure pattern analysis across production periods
- **Supplier performance**: Quality trends en supplier accountability

**Quality investigation support:**
- **Root cause analysis**: Complete history voor [[corrective-and-preventive-action|CAPA]] investigations
- **Customer complaints**: Rapid response met complete product history
- **Field failures**: Correlation tussen production conditions en performance
- **Process improvement**: Historical data analysis voor optimization opportunities

**Regulatory compliance:**
- **Audit trails**: Complete documentation voor regulatory inspections
- **Material certificates**: Mill test certificates, chemical composition records
- **Process validation**: Evidence van [[controleplan|control plan]] execution
- **Change management**: [[engineering-change-order|ECO]] implementation verification

**Digital genealogy systems:**
- **[[manufacturing-execution-system|MES]] integration**: Real-time genealogy data capture
- **Blockchain tracking**: Immutable record keeping voor critical applications
- **Cloud-based systems**: Scalable genealogy data management
- **Mobile access**: Real-time genealogy query capabilities
