---
*Industrieel metaversum (industrial metaverse)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en innovatie & toekomsttrends.

## Definitie

**Industrieel metaversum (industrial metaverse)** is een virtuele omgeving waarin fysieke industriële processen, faciliteiten en systemen worden gerepresenteerd door immersieve [[digital-twin|digital twins]]. Het combineert [[virtual-reality|VR]], [[augmented-reality|AR]], [[artificial-intelligence|AI]] en real-time data om collaborative virtual workspaces te creëren voor design, training, maintenance en operations.

## Kenmerken

- **Immersive environments**: VR/AR interfaces voor 3D industrial environment interaction
- **Real-time synchronization**: Bi-directional data exchange tussen virtual en physical assets
- **Collaborative spaces**: Multi-user virtual environments voor cross-functional teams
- **Persistent virtual worlds**: Continuous virtual representation van industrial facilities
- **Physics simulation**: Accurate modeling van mechanical, thermal, fluid dynamics
- **AI integration**: Intelligent virtual assistants en automated process optimization
- **Cross-platform access**: Support voor verschillende VR/AR devices en platforms
- **Spatial computing**: 3D user interface en gesture-based interaction
- **Digital asset libraries**: Reusable virtual components en equipment models

## Toepassing

**Design en engineering:**
- **Virtual prototyping**: 3D product design review en testing in virtual environments
- **[[computer-aided-design|CAD]] collaboration**: Multi-user design sessions in shared virtual spaces
- **Factory layout**: Virtual facility planning en optimization
- **Equipment placement**: 3D visualization van machine positioning en workflow

**Training en education:**
- **Safety training**: Hazard simulation zonder real-world risks
- **Equipment operation**: Virtual machine operation training programmes
- **Maintenance procedures**: Step-by-step virtual maintenance instruction
- **[[lean-manufacturing|Process training]]**: Virtual value stream mapping en process improvement

**Remote operations:**
- **Virtual inspections**: Remote facility monitoring via immersive interfaces
- **[[predictive-maintenance|Maintenance support]]**: AR-guided repair procedures
- **Expert assistance**: Remote expert guidance through virtual presence
- **Quality control**: Virtual measurement en inspection processes

**Production optimization:**
- **[[digital-twin|Digital twin]] integration**: Virtual-physical process synchronization
- **[[roboticasimulatiesoftware|Robot programming]]**: Virtual robot path programming en testing
- **Production simulation**: Virtual production line testing en optimization
- **Material flow**: Virtual warehouse en logistics optimization

**Collaboration en communication:**
- **Global team meetings**: Virtual factory tours en stakeholder presentations
- **Design reviews**: Immersive product en process review sessions
- **Knowledge transfer**: Virtual apprenticeship en skill development
- **Customer engagement**: Virtual showrooms en product demonstrations
