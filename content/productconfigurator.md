*Productconfigurator* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en productstructuur & documentatie.

## Definitie

**Productconfigurator** is een software tool die klanten en verkopers in staat stelt om complexe, geconfigureerde producten samen te stellen door het selecteren van opties, specificaties en varianten. Het systeem valideert automatisch de compatibiliteit van gekozen combinaties en genereert correcte [[manufacturing-bom|mBOM]], prijzen en technische documentatie.

## Kenmerken

- **Rules-based configuration**: Automatische validatie van geldige product combinaties
- **Visual interface**: Grafische representatie van configuratie keuzes en resultaten
- **Constraint management**: Incompatible options worden automatisch uitgesloten
- **Dynamic pricing**: Real-time cost calculation op basis van gekozen configuratie
- **[[manufacturing-bom|BOM]] generation**: Automatische creatie van productiestructuur
- **Documentation output**: Tekeningen, specificaties en [[werkinstructie|work instructions]]
- **[[configure-price-quote|CPQ]] integratie**: Seamless quoting en order processing
- **Multi-language support**: Internationale verkoop en configuratie ondersteuning

## Toepassing

**Metaalproduct configuratie:**
- **Machined components**: Material selection, dimensions, tolerances, surface finishes
- **Welded structures**: Base configurations, reinforcement options, coating selections
- **Sheet metal products**: Gauge thickness, bend configurations, perforation patterns
- **Assembly systems**: Component selections, mounting options, accessory packages

**Industrial equipment configurators:**
- **Machine tools**: Spindle options, tool changers, control systems, safety features
- **Conveyor systems**: Belt types, lengths, drive configurations, control integration
- **Robotic cells**: Robot models, tooling options, safety systems, programming packages
- **Hydraulic systems**: Pump selections, valve configurations, filtration options

**Configuration rules engine:**
- **Compatibility rules**: Ensure selected options work together technically
- **Business rules**: Pricing tiers, availability, lead time calculations
- **Manufacturing constraints**: Producibility validation, tooling requirements
- **Quality requirements**: Automatic inclusion van required [[controleplan|control features]]

**[[configure-price-quote|CPQ]] workflow:**
- **Customer selection**: Guided configuration process met visual feedback
- **Technical validation**: Engineering rules checking voor feasibility
- **Cost calculation**: Material, labor en overhead cost roll-up
- **Quote generation**: Professional proposal documents met specifications
- **Order processing**: Direct transfer naar [[enterprise-resource-planning|ERP]] voor production

**Metaalindustrie configuratie voorbeelden:**
- **Structural steel**: Beam sizes, connection types, coating specifications
- **Pressure vessels**: Material grades, wall thickness, nozzle configurations
- **Custom tooling**: Cutting tools, fixtures, measurement equipment
- **Heat exchangers**: Tube configurations, baffle designs, connection options

**Engineering integration:**
- **[[computer-aided-design|CAD]] automation**: Parametric model generation van configuraties
- **[[engineering-bom|eBOM]] creation**: Design structure generation
- **Drawing automation**: Technical drawing production van configuraties
- **Documentation linking**: Specifications, procedures, compliance documents
