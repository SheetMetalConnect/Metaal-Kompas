*Traceerbaarheidssysteem* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en traceerbaarheid & productidentificatie.

## Definitie

**Traceerbaarheidssysteem** is een geintegreerde IT-infrastructuur die volledige transparantie biedt over de herkomst, verwerking en distributie van materialen en producten door de gehele supply chain. Het systeem combineert identificatie-technologieën, data management en analytics om real-time inzicht te geven in product genealogie en compliance status.

## Kenmerken

- **End-to-end visibility**: Complete supply chain traceability van grondstof tot eindklant
- **Multi-tier tracking**: Forward en backward tracing capabilities
- **Real-time data capture**: Live updates van product status en locatie
- **Integration capability**: Koppeling met [[manufacturing-execution-system|MES]], [[enterprise-resource-planning|ERP]], quality systems
- **Compliance automation**: Automated regulatory reporting en documentation
- **Exception management**: Alert generation bij afwijkingen of non-compliance
- **Historical archiving**: Long-term data retention voor audit en analysis
- **Scalable architecture**: Support voor multiple plants, suppliers, customers

## Toepassing

**Manufacturing traceability:**
- **[[lot-tracking|Lot tracking]]**: Material batches door complete productieproces
- **Assembly genealogy**: Component-to-product relationships en history
- **Process documentation**: Step-by-step manufacturing history per product
- **Quality integration**: Test results, inspections linked to specific products

**Supply chain visibility:**
- **Supplier traceability**: Raw material sources, certifications, test data
- **Transportation tracking**: Logistics en custody chain documentation
- **Customer delivery**: Product information available to end customers
- **Returns management**: Efficient product recall en warranty handling

**Regulatory compliance:**
- **FDA compliance**: Food, pharmaceutical, medical device traceability
- **Automotive standards**: IATF 16949, AIAG traceability requirements
- **Aerospace**: AS9100, NADCAP compliance documentation
- **ISO certification**: ISO 9001 traceability requirements

**Digital transformation:**
- **[[industrial-internet-of-things|IIoT]] integration**: Connected sensors, scanners, readers
- **Blockchain traceability**: Immutable product history records
- **AI-powered analytics**: Pattern recognition, predictive insights
- **Mobile access**: Smartphone/tablet traceability applications
