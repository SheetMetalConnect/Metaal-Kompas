*IO‑Link* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en industriële communicatieprotocollen.

## Definitie

**IO-Link** is een open industrieel communicatieprotocol dat intelligente sensoren en actuatoren verbindt met automatiseringssystemen via een simple point-to-point verbinding. Het enables parametrisatie, diagnostiek en data-uitwisseling met standaard onafgeschermde kabels.

## Kenmerken

- **Point-to-point connection**: Direct communication tussen master en device
- **Standard cabling**: Unshielded 3-wire cables (power, communication, ground)
- **Device parametrization**: Remote configuration van sensor/actuator parameters
- **Diagnostic capabilities**: Real-time device health monitoring en error reporting
- **Hot-swappable devices**: Replacement zonder system downtime
- **Vendor independence**: Open standard supporting multiple manufacturers
- **Backwards compatibility**: Support voor traditional digital/analog devices
- **Auto-configuration**: Automatic device recognition en parameter download
- **Data transparency**: Bi-directional data exchange tussen fieldbus levels

## Toepassing

**Sensor applications:**
- **Proximity sensors**: Position detection in metal fabrication machinery
- **Temperature sensors**: Heat monitoring in steel processing furnaces
- **Pressure sensors**: Hydraulic system monitoring in metal forming presses
- **Flow sensors**: Coolant flow measurement in CNC machining centers

**Actuator control:**
- **Pneumatic valves**: Controlled actuation in automated assembly lines
- **Electric actuators**: Precise positioning in welding robot systems
- **Motor starters**: Remote control van conveyor belt drives
- **Safety valves**: Emergency shutdown systems in metal processing

**Machine integration:**
- **CNC machine tools**: Tool condition monitoring en automatic tool changers
- **Robotic welding**: Weld parameter adjustment en quality monitoring
- **Material handling**: Automated guided vehicle (AGV) sensor integration
- **Packaging equipment**: Product detection en counting in metal goods packaging

**Maintenance benefits:**
- **Predictive maintenance**: Early warning systems voor equipment failures
- **Remote diagnostics**: Troubleshooting without physical access to devices
- **Parameter backup**: Automatic restoration van device settings after replacement
- **Performance monitoring**: Continuous tracking van device operational parameters

**System advantages:**
- **Reduced wiring**: Single cable voor power en communication
- **Easy commissioning**: Plug-and-play device installation
- **Flexible topology**: Simple star configuration from IO-Link masters
- **Cost efficiency**: Lower installation en maintenance costs
