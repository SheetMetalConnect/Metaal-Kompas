*Master Data Management (MDM)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en data, analyse & business intelligence.

## Definitie

**Master Data Management (MDM)** zorgt voor consistente, accurate en gezaghebbende master data (zoals klanten, producten, leveranciers) door alle systemen heen. Het elimineert data-duplicatie en waarborgt één bron van waarheid voor kritieke bedrijfsentiteiten.

## Kenmerken

- **Single source of truth**: Één gezaghebbende bron voor kritieke master data
- **Data consistency**: Uniforme data definities across all enterprise systems
- **Data quality**: Automated validation, cleansing en standardization
- **Governance framework**: Policies, procedures en accountability voor data management
- **Real-time synchronization**: Immediate updates across connected systems
- **Hierarchy management**: Complex organizational en product hierarchies
- **Workflow integration**: Approval processes voor data changes en updates
- **Data lineage**: Tracking van data origins en transformations
- **Access control**: Role-based permissions voor data viewing en editing

## Toepassing

**Product master data:**
- **Part number management**: Centralized part numbering voor components en assemblies
- **Bill of materials**: [[manufacturing-bom|BOM]] consistency across engineering en manufacturing
- **Product specifications**: Technical specifications, drawings, certifications
- **Inventory data**: Stock levels, locations, reorder points synchronization

**Customer master data:**
- **Customer profiles**: Unified customer information across sales, service, finance
- **Account hierarchies**: Complex customer relationships en organizational structures
- **Contact management**: Sales contacts, technical contacts, financial contacts
- **Pricing data**: Customer-specific pricing, contracts, terms

**Supplier master data:**
- **Vendor information**: Supplier details, capabilities, certifications
- **Performance metrics**: Quality ratings, delivery performance, cost data
- **Contract management**: Terms, conditions, pricing agreements
- **Qualification status**: Approved vendor lists, audit results

**Asset master data:**
- **Equipment registry**: Machine specifications, serial numbers, locations
- **Maintenance data**: Service schedules, parts lists, performance history
- **Financial data**: Asset values, depreciation, cost centers
- **Documentation**: Manuals, warranties, service contracts

**Financial master data:**
- **Chart of accounts**: Consistent accounting structures across business units
- **Cost centers**: Organizational structure voor cost allocation
- **Budget data**: Planning data synchronized across systems
- **Currency management**: Exchange rates, multi-currency transactions
