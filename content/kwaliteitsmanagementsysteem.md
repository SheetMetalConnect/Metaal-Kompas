*Kwaliteitsmanagementsysteem (Quality Management System, QMS)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en kwaliteitsmanagement & software.

## Definitie

**Kwaliteitsmanagementsysteem (QMS)** is een samenhangend set van gedocumenteerde processen, procedures en verantwoordelijkheden die ervoor zorgen dat een organisatie consistent producten en diensten levert die voldoen aan klant- en regulatoire eisen. Het vormt de backbone voor systematic quality assurance in de metaalindustrie.

## Kenmerken

- **Process-based approach**: Systematic identification en management van business processes
- **Risk-based thinking**: Proactive identification en mitigation van quality risks
- **Customer focus**: Alignment van alle processen met customer requirements
- **[[continuous-improvement|Continuous improvement]]**: Systematic enhancement van processes en outcomes
- **Evidence-based decisions**: Data-driven quality management decisions
- **Leadership engagement**: Top management commitment en involvement
- **People involvement**: Employee empowerment en competency development
- **Supplier relationship management**: Quality assurance throughout supply chain

## Toepassing

**[[iso-9001-kwaliteitsmanagement|ISO 9001]] implementatie:**
- **Context analysis**: Understanding van organizational context en stakeholder needs
- **Quality policy**: Top-level commitment naar quality excellence
- **Quality objectives**: Measurable goals aligned met business strategy
- **Process documentation**: Procedures, [[werkinstructie|work instructions]], forms

**Metaalindustrie QMS elementen:**
- **[[controleplan|Control plans]]**: Quality control measures per production process
- **[[inspectieplan|Inspection plans]]**: Systematic product quality verification
- **[[corrective-and-preventive-action|CAPA]] system**: Systematic problem solving en prevention
- **[[non-conformance-report|NCR]] management**: Nonconformity identification en resolution

**Document control system:**
- **Document hierarchy**: Quality manual, procedures, work instructions
- **[[revisiebeheer|Revision control]]**: Version management en change control
- **Distribution control**: Ensuring correct documents at right locations
- **Record management**: Quality records retention en retrieval

**Audit en assessment:**
- **Internal audits**: Systematic QMS effectiveness verification
- **Management review**: Regular QMS performance assessment
- **Customer audits**: External verification van quality capabilities
- **Certification audits**: Third-party QMS compliance assessment

**Digital QMS integration:**
- **[[document-management-system|DMS]]**: Electronic document control systems
- **[[statistical-process-control-software|SPC]] integration**: Quality data within QMS framework
- **[[manufacturing-execution-system|MES]] alignment**: Production quality control integration
- **Training management**: Competency tracking en training effectiveness

**Metaalbewerking specific applications:**
- **Welding quality**: [[welding-procedure-specification|WPS]], [[procedure-qualification-record|PQR]], welder qualification management
- **Material control**: Incoming inspection, [[heat-number|heat number]] tracking, material certificates
- **[[first-article-inspection|FAI]]**: New product introduction quality validation
- **Calibration system**: Measuring equipment accuracy assurance
