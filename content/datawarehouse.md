*Datawarehouse* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en data, analyse & business intelligence.

## Definitie

**Datawarehouse** is een gecentraliseerde repository die gestructureerde data uit verschillende bronnen integreert en organiseert voor rapportage en analyse. Het bevat historische data die is getransformeerd en geoptimaliseerd voor query performance en business intelligence.

## Kenmerken

- **Subject-oriented**: Organized around business subjects rather than operational processes
- **Integrated**: Consistent data from multiple heterogeneous sources
- **Time-variant**: Historical data storage spanning multiple time periods
- **Non-volatile**: Stable data repository that doesn't change frequently
- **Normalized structure**: Optimized database schemas voor analytical queries
- **High performance**: Indexing en partitioning voor fast query execution
- **Metadata management**: Documentation van data lineage en business definitions
- **Data quality**: Cleansed en validated data voor reliable analytics
- **Scalability**: Ability to handle growing data volumes en user loads

## Toepassing

**Manufacturing analytics:**
- **Production metrics**: Historical production data analysis voor trend identification
- **Quality tracking**: Defect rates en quality measures across time periods
- **Equipment performance**: Machine utilization en downtime analysis
- **Cost analysis**: Manufacturing cost trends en efficiency measurements

**Supply chain intelligence:**
- **Inventory analysis**: Stock level trends en demand forecasting
- **Supplier performance**: Vendor delivery times en quality metrics
- **Demand planning**: Historical sales data voor production scheduling
- **Logistics optimization**: Transportation costs en delivery performance

**Financial reporting:**
- **Management accounting**: Cost center performance en budget variance analysis
- **Revenue analysis**: Sales trends by product, region, customer segments
- **Profitability analysis**: Product line en customer profitability insights
- **Regulatory reporting**: Financial data voor compliance requirements

**Customer analytics:**
- **Sales performance**: Customer purchasing patterns en lifetime value
- **Market analysis**: Regional sales trends en market share analysis
- **Customer segmentation**: Behavioral analysis voor targeted marketing
- **Service analytics**: Support metrics en customer satisfaction trends

**Operational intelligence:**
- **KPI dashboards**: Key performance indicators tracking across departments
- **Resource utilization**: Workforce en asset utilization analysis
- **Process analytics**: Workflow efficiency en bottleneck identification
- **Compliance monitoring**: Regulatory requirement tracking en reporting
