*Datagovernance* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en data, analyse & business intelligence.

## Definitie

**Datagovernance** is het framework van policies, procedures, rollen en verantwoordelijkheden dat de effective management van data assets binnen een organisatie waarborgt. Het omvat [[data-kwaliteitsbeheer|data quality]], security, privacy, compliance en lifecycle management om data als strategic business asset te beheren.

## Kenmerken

- **Data stewardship**: Designated ownership en accountability voor specific data domains
- **Policy framework**: Comprehensive data policies, standards en procedures
- **Quality assurance**: [[data-kwaliteitsbeheer|Data quality]] measurement, monitoring, improvement processes
- **Access control**: Role-based data access permissions en security measures
- **Compliance management**: Regulatory adherence (GDPR, industry-specific requirements)
- **[[metadata-beheer|Metadata management]]**: Data cataloging, lineage tracking, documentation
- **Risk management**: Data-related risk identification, assessment, mitigation
- **Lifecycle management**: Data retention, archival, disposal policies
- **Change control**: Data structure en process change approval workflows

## Toepassing

**Manufacturing data governance:**
- **[[historian-database|Process data]]**: Time-series data retention, quality, access policies
- **Quality data**: [[kwaliteitsdata-analyse-tools|Measurement]] data governance, traceability requirements
- **Production metrics**: [[key-performance-indicator|KPI]] definition standardization en calculation consistency
- **Equipment data**: Asset information governance, maintenance data policies

**[[master-data-management|Master data]] governance:**
- **Product data**: Part numbers, specifications, revision control governance
- **Supplier data**: Vendor information accuracy, completeness, access control
- **Customer data**: Account data privacy, consent management, retention policies
- **Material data**: Chemical compositions, certifications, regulatory compliance

**Regulatory compliance:**
- **Traceability**: Material en process data retention voor regulatory requirements
- **Quality records**: [[iso-9001-kwaliteitsmanagementsysteem|ISO]] compliance data management
- **Environmental data**: Emissions, waste, energy consumption reporting governance
- **Safety data**: Incident reporting, risk assessment data management

**[[business-intelligence|Business intelligence]] governance:**
- **Report definitions**: Standardized metrics, calculation methodologies
- **[[data-warehousing|Data warehouse]]**: ETL process governance, data integration policies
- **Analytics governance**: Model development standards, validation procedures
- **Self-service analytics**: End-user data access policies, training requirements

**Data security en privacy:**
- **Access management**: Role-based permissions, privileged access controls
- **Data classification**: Sensitivity levels, handling requirements
- **Privacy compliance**: Personal data protection, consent management
- **Incident response**: Data breach procedures, notification requirements
