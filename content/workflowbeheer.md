*Workflowbeheer* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en productstructuur & documentatie.

## Definitie

**Workflowbeheer** is het systematische ontwerpen, implementeren, uitvoeren en monitoren van geautomatiseerde bedrijfsprocessen waarbij taken, documenten en informatie tussen personen, systemen en afdelingen bewegen volgens voorgedefinieerde regels en procedures. Het digitaliseert en optimaliseert handmatige workflows voor verhoogde efficiëntie en consistentie.

## Kenmerken

- **Process automation**: Geautomatiseerde taak routing en execution
- **Rule-based logic**: Conditionele flow control gebaseerd op business rules
- **Task assignment**: Automatische work allocation naar appropriate resources
- **Status tracking**: Real-time visibility in workflow progress en bottlenecks
- **Approval workflows**: Multi-level approval processes met escalation mechanisms
- **Integration capabilities**: Connectivity met [[enterprise-resource-planning|ERP]], [[document-management-system|DMS]] systems
- **Audit trails**: Complete workflow history en compliance documentation
- **Exception handling**: Automatic en manual intervention capabilities
- **Performance metrics**: Workflow efficiency measurement en optimization

## Toepassing

**Document approval processes:**
- **[[engineering-change-order|ECO]] workflows**: Engineering change request routing en approval
- **[[controleplan|Control plan]]**: Quality document review en release processes
- **Technical drawings**: [[computer-aided-design|CAD]] document approval workflows
- **[[projectdocumentatie|Project documentation]]**: Deliverable review en sign-off processes

**Manufacturing workflows:**
- **[[manufacturing-bom|BOM]] approval**: Bill of materials review en release workflows
- **Production orders**: [[manufacturing-execution-system|MES]] work order approval en routing
- **Quality workflows**: [[non-conformance-report|NCR]] investigation en corrective action processes
- **Maintenance requests**: [[computerized-maintenance-management-system|CMMS]] work order approval en scheduling

**Procurement processes:**
- **Purchase requisitions**: Automated approval routing gebaseerd on spending limits
- **Vendor qualification**: Supplier evaluation en approval workflows
- **Contract reviews**: Legal en commercial contract approval processes
- **Invoice processing**: Accounts payable approval workflows

**Quality management:**
- **[[corrective-and-preventive-action|CAPA]] processes**: Issue investigation en resolution workflows
- **Audit management**: Internal audit scheduling, execution, en follow-up
- **Customer complaints**: Complaint handling en response workflows
- **Supplier quality**: Vendor performance review en improvement processes

**Human resources:**
- **Employee onboarding**: New hire documentation en training workflows
- **Performance reviews**: Annual evaluation process automation
- **Training management**: Skill development en certification workflows
- **Leave requests**: Vacation en absence approval processes
