*Mens‑robot samenwerking (HRC)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en digitale transformatie & industrie 4.0/5.0.

## Definitie

**Mens-robot samenwerking (HRC - Human-Robot Collaboration)** beschrijft directe interactie tussen mensen en robots in een gedeelde werkruimte. Robots assisteren mensen bij taken waarbij menselijke intelligentie en robot-precisie worden gecombineerd voor optimale resultaten.

## Kenmerken

- **Collaborative safety**: Advanced sensors voor collision detection en force limiting
- **Intuitive interaction**: Natural communication through gestures, voice, touch
- **Adaptive behavior**: Robot learning from human preferences en work patterns
- **Flexible programming**: Easy reconfiguration voor different tasks en operators
- **Shared workspace**: Safe simultaneous operation in same physical area
- **Force sensing**: Precise force feedback voor delicate assembly operations
- **Vision systems**: Real-time monitoring van human movements en intentions
- **Emergency stops**: Immediate response systems voor safety incidents
- **Learning capability**: Continuous improvement through human-robot interaction

## Toepassing

**Assembly operations:**
- **Component handling**: Robot positions parts while human performs precision assembly
- **Quality inspection**: Shared visual inspection met human judgment en robot measurement
- **Fastener installation**: Robot holds components while human applies torque
- **Complex assembly**: Combining robot strength met human dexterity voor intricate parts

**Metalworking applications:**
- **Sheet metal bending**: Robot positions material while operator guides bend operations
- **Welding assistance**: Robot holds workpieces while human performs precision welds
- **Material handling**: Collaborative lifting van heavy steel components
- **Finishing operations**: Robot positioning voor human polishing en deburring

**Safety-critical tasks:**
- **Hazardous material handling**: Robot exposure reduction while maintaining human oversight
- **Heavy lifting**: Shared load bearing voor injury prevention
- **Repetitive motions**: Robot performs repetitive tasks while human handles exceptions
- **Precision operations**: Human oversight van robot precision work

**Training en skills transfer:**
- **Apprentice support**: Robots assist new workers during learning process
- **Knowledge capture**: Recording human techniques voor robot learning
- **Skill augmentation**: Enhancing human capabilities through robot assistance
- **Process improvement**: Collaborative optimization van work methods

**Flexible manufacturing:**
- **Batch production**: Quick reconfiguration voor different product variants
- **Custom orders**: Human judgment combined with robot consistency
- **Prototype development**: Iterative collaboration voor new product development
- **Quality control**: Human inspection supported by robot measurements
