*Generative design (generatief ontwerp)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en innovatie & toekomsttrends.

## Definitie

**Generative design (generatief ontwerp)** gebruikt AI-algoritmes om automatisch ontwerpalternatieven te genereren op basis van opgegeven eisen en beperkingen. Het exploreert duizenden ontwerpopties om optimale oplossingen te vinden voor gewicht, sterkte, materiaalgebruik en productiekosten.

## Kenmerken

- **AI-driven optimization**: Machine learning algorithms exploring design space
- **Multi-objective optimization**: Balancing weight, strength, cost, manufacturability simultaneously
- **Constraint-based generation**: Designs generated within specified manufacturing limits
- **Biomimetic forms**: Organic shapes inspired by natural structures
- **Material optimization**: Efficient material distribution voor structural performance
- **Topology optimization**: Internal structure optimization voor load paths
- **Manufacturing constraints**: Design generation considering production methods
- **Performance simulation**: Integrated FEA analysis during design generation
- **Iterative refinement**: Continuous improvement through learning algorithms

## Toepassing

**Aerospace components:**
- **Aircraft brackets**: Lightweight structural components met complex geometries
- **Engine mounts**: Optimized load transfer structures voor jet engines
- **Heat exchangers**: Complex internal channels voor efficient thermal management
- **Turbine blades**: Aerodynamic optimization met material efficiency

**Automotive applications:**
- **Chassis components**: Lightweight frames maintaining crash safety requirements
- **Engine components**: Pistons, connecting rods optimized voor performance
- **Heat management**: Cooling system components met optimized flow paths
- **Electric vehicle**: Battery brackets, motor housings designed voor efficiency

**Manufacturing tooling:**
- **Injection molds**: Cooling channels optimized voor cycle time reduction
- **Machining fixtures**: Lightweight workholding devices met maximum rigidity
- **Cutting tools**: Tool geometries optimized voor specific materials
- **Assembly jigs**: Custom fixtures generated voor specific production runs

**Metal industry applications:**
- **Steel connections**: Optimized structural joints voor building construction
- **Heat sink design**: Complex fin geometries voor thermal management
- **Pressure vessels**: Internal structures optimized voor stress distribution
- **Forging dies**: Complex geometries optimized voor material flow

**Additive manufacturing:**
- **3D printed parts**: Complex internal structures impossible with traditional manufacturing
- **Lattice structures**: Lightweight designs met tailored mechanical properties
- **Support optimization**: Minimal support structures reducing post-processing
- **Multi-material design**: Optimized material placement in hybrid structures
