*Variantbeheer* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en productstructuur & documentatie.

## Definitie

**Variantbeheer** is het systematische management van productvariaties, configuraties en opties binnen een productfamilie. Het omvat de planning, structurering en beheersing van verschillende productversies, modellen en specificaties om [[mass-customization|mass customization]] mogelijk te maken terwijl complexiteit en kosten beheersbaar blijven.

## Kenmerken

- **Product family structure**: Hierarchical organization van base products en variants
- **Configuration rules**: Business rules governing valid product combinations
- **Option management**: Systematic control van features, accessories, specifications
- **[[manufacturing-bom|BOM]] variant control**: Managing multiple bill of materials versions
- **Cost transparency**: Variant costing en profitability analysis
- **Lead time management**: Production planning per variant configuration
- **Documentation control**: Variant-specific drawings, specifications, instructions
- **Change impact analysis**: Effect van [[engineering-change-order|ECO]] across variants

## Toepassing

**Metaalproduct variants:**
- **Machine tool variations**: Different spindle options, chuck sizes, control systems
- **Structural steel**: Beam lengths, connection types, coating options
- **Pressure vessels**: Wall thickness, nozzle configurations, material grades
- **Custom enclosures**: Size variations, mounting options, finish specifications

**Manufacturing variant management:**
- **[[manufacturing-bom|BOM]] variants**: Multiple production structures per product family
- **[[productierouting|Routing]] variants**: Different manufacturing processes per configuration
- **Quality variants**: [[controleplan|Control plan]] variations per specification level
- **Tooling variants**: Fixture en tooling requirements per product variant

**Configuration management:**
- **[[productconfigurator|Product configurator]]**: Customer-facing configuration tools
- **Valid combinations**: Rules preventing incompatible option selections
- **Pricing matrix**: Variant-specific cost calculations
- **Availability management**: Lead times en stock status per variant

**Engineering challenges:**
- **Commonality analysis**: Maximizing shared components across variants
- **Design modularity**: Modular design enabling efficient variant creation
- **Testing requirements**: Validation needs per unique configuration
- **Documentation explosion**: Managing multiple documents per variant

**Digital variant management:**
- **[[product-lifecycle-management|PLM]] systems**: Variant structure management
- **[[enterprise-resource-planning|ERP]] integration**: Business process variant handling
- **[[configure-price-quote|CPQ]] tools**: Sales configuration en quoting systems
- **Manufacturing execution**: [[manufacturing-execution-system|MES]] variant production control

**Complexity management strategies:**
- **Modular design**: Reducing variants through modular architecture
- **Postponement**: Delaying final configuration until customer order
- **Platform strategy**: Shared platforms with variant differentiation
- **Value stream mapping**: Identifying variant complexity costs
