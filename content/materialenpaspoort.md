*Materialenpaspoort* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en duurzaamheid & compliance.

## Definitie

**Materialenpaspoort (Materials Passport)** is een digitaal document dat comprehensive informatie bevat over de samenstelling, properties, herkomst en lifecycle van materialen in producten. Het ondersteunt [[circulaire-economie|circular economy]] initiatieven door traceability, recyclability en sustainability informatie te documenteren voor informed material decisions.

## Kenmerken

- **Material composition**: Detailed breakdown van chemical composition en material types
- **Supply chain traceability**: Origin tracking van raw materials through suppliers
- **Environmental impact**: Carbon footprint, energy consumption, sustainability metrics
- **Quality specifications**: Mechanical properties, test results, certifications
- **Lifecycle information**: Usage history, maintenance records, performance data
- **Recyclability data**: End-of-life options, recycling procedures, material recovery potential
- **Compliance documentation**: Regulatory compliance, safety data sheets, certifications
- **Digital identity**: Unique identifiers, QR codes, blockchain-based authenticity
- **Interoperability**: Standardized data formats voor cross-system compatibility

## Toepassing

**Metaalindustrie implementatie:**
- **Steel passports**: Chemical composition, mechanical properties, mill test certificates
- **Aluminum traceability**: Alloy specifications, recycled content, processing history
- **Copper documentation**: Purity levels, recycling history, electrical properties
- **Specialty alloys**: High-performance material specifications en quality certifications

**Manufacturing applications:**
- **Component tracking**: Material properties throughout [[manufacturing-bom|product lifecycle]]
- **Quality assurance**: Material test results, certifications, compliance documentation
- **Supplier verification**: Material source verification en quality attestation
- **Inventory management**: Material identification, specifications, storage requirements

**[[circulaire-economie|Circular economy]] support:**
- **End-of-life planning**: Material recovery instructions, recycling procedures
- **Remanufacturing**: Component condition assessment, refurbishment feasibility
- **Material banking**: High-value material identification voor future recovery
- **Waste reduction**: Material optimization based on composition en performance data

**Regulatory compliance:**
- **REACH regulation**: Chemical substance registration, safety information
- **RoHS compliance**: Restricted substance verification, environmental compliance
- **Conflict minerals**: Supply chain due diligence, responsible sourcing verification
- **Product liability**: Material safety documentation, usage limitations

**Digital integration:**
- **[[blockchain-traceability|Blockchain tracking]]**: Immutable material provenance records
- **[[industrial-internet-of-things|IoT]] integration**: Real-time material condition monitoring
- **[[artificial-intelligence|AI]] analytics**: Material performance prediction, optimization recommendations
- **[[digital-twin|Digital twin]]**: Virtual material representation met real-time data
