title: Digitale servitisatie (digital servitization)
---
*Digitale servitisatie (digital servitization)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en innovatie & toekomsttrends.

## Definitie

**Digitale servitisatie (digital servitization)** is de business transformation waarbij manufacturing companies hun product-focused business models uitbreiden met digitaal-enabled service offerings. Het integreert [[digital-twin|digital twins]], [[industrial-internet-of-things|IoT]], data analytics en connected products om nieuwe revenue streams en customer value propositions te creëren.

## Kenmerken

- **Product-service integration**: Combining physical products met digital service capabilities
- **Data monetization**: Creating value from product usage data en customer insights
- **Outcome-based business models**: Performance-based contracts en service level agreements
- **Connected products**: [[industrial-internet-of-things|IoT]]-enabled products generating service opportunities
- **Predictive capabilities**: [[predictive-maintenance|Proactive service]] delivery based on data analytics
- **Customer engagement**: Ongoing digital relationships beyond product sale
- **Service platforms**: Digital platforms enabling scalable service delivery
- **Lifecycle services**: Services spanning entire product lifecycle from design to disposal
- **Digital touchpoints**: Multiple digital channels voor customer interaction

## Toepassing

**Manufacturing equipment servitization:**
- **[[predictive-maintenance|Equipment monitoring]]**: Remote diagnostics en predictive maintenance services
- **Performance optimization**: Data-driven optimization services voor production equipment
- **Training services**: Digital training platforms voor equipment operation
- **Spare parts optimization**: Predictive parts replacement en inventory optimization

**Product-as-a-service models:**
- **Equipment leasing**: Pay-per-use business models voor manufacturing equipment
- **Outcome-based contracts**: Guaranteed production output rather than equipment sales
- **Subscription services**: Ongoing service subscriptions voor product functionality
- **Shared equipment**: Multi-tenant equipment usage models

**Digital service platforms:**
- **Customer portals**: Self-service platforms voor order management, support
- **Mobile applications**: Field service apps, remote monitoring dashboards
- **API ecosystems**: Platform APIs enabling third-party service integration
- **Marketplace platforms**: Digital marketplaces connecting services met customers

**Data-driven services:**
- **Usage analytics**: Product utilization reporting en optimization recommendations
- **Benchmarking services**: Performance comparison tegen industry standards
- **Compliance reporting**: Automated regulatory reporting based on product data
- **Energy optimization**: Efficiency improvement services based on consumption data

**Customer experience enhancement:**
- **Digital onboarding**: Streamlined customer setup en training processes
- **Proactive support**: Issue prevention through monitoring en early intervention
- **Personalized experiences**: Customized service delivery based on usage patterns
- **Continuous improvement**: Service enhancement based on customer feedback loops
