*ISO 14001‑milieumanagement* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en kwaliteitsmanagement & software.

## Definitie

**ISO 14001-milieumanagement** is een internationale standaard voor milieumanagementsystemen (EMS) die organisaties helpt hun milieu-impact te verminderen, regelgeving na te leven en [[continuous-improvement|continue verbetering]] te realiseren op milieugebied. Voor de metaalindustrie biedt het een systematische aanpak voor duurzame productie en resource efficiency.

## Kenmerken

- **Environmental Management System**: Systematische aanpak voor milieubeheer
- **Life cycle thinking**: Milieuimpact assessment door complete product lifecycle
- **Legal compliance**: Structured approach voor environmental regulations
- **Risk-based approach**: Proactive identification van environmental risks
- **Stakeholder engagement**: Communication met internal/external stakeholders
- **Performance monitoring**: KPI tracking en environmental metrics
- **[[continuous-improvement|Continuous improvement]]**: Ongoing enhancement van environmental performance
- **Integration capability**: Alignment met [[iso-9001-kwaliteitsmanagement|ISO 9001]], [[iso-45001-veiligheid|ISO 45001]]

## Toepassing

**Metaalindustrie milieubeheer:**
- **Energy management**: Reduction van energy consumption in machining, heating processes
- **Waste reduction**: Scrap minimization, recycling programs, circular economy initiatives
- **Emissions control**: Air quality management, CO2 reduction, volatile organic compounds
- **Water management**: Coolant recycling, wastewater treatment, consumption reduction

**Manufacturing processes:**
- **CNC machining**: Coolant optimization, chip recycling, energy-efficient cutting parameters
- **Heat treatment**: Furnace efficiency, emissions control, energy recovery systems
- **Surface finishing**: Chemical usage reduction, waste treatment, VOC minimization
- **Welding processes**: Fume extraction, electrode waste management, energy optimization

**Supply chain environmental management:**
- **Supplier assessment**: Environmental criteria in supplier selection
- **Material sourcing**: Sustainable steel sourcing, conflict minerals avoidance
- **Transportation**: Logistics optimization voor carbon footprint reduction
- **Packaging**: Reduction van packaging waste, sustainable materials

**Digital environmental tools:**
- **[[energy-management-systemen|Energy management systems]]**: Real-time energy monitoring en optimization
- **[[carbon-footprint|Carbon footprint]] tracking**: Automated CO2 calculation en reporting
- **Waste tracking systems**: Digital waste stream monitoring en reporting
- **Environmental reporting**: Automated compliance reporting en KPI dashboards

**Resource efficiency:**
- **Material optimization**: Raw material waste minimization through better planning
- **Process efficiency**: [[lean-manufacturing|Lean]] principles voor environmental waste reduction
- **Equipment optimization**: Energy-efficient machinery selection en maintenance
- **Circular economy**: Material reuse, recycling programs, byproduct utilization

**Regulatory compliance:**
- **Environmental permits**: Management van air, water, waste permits
- **Reporting requirements**: Automated generation van regulatory reports  
- **Audit preparation**: Internal en external environmental audit readiness
- **Incident management**: Environmental incident tracking en corrective action
