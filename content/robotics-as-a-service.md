*Robotics as a Service (RaaS)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en innovatie & toekomsttrends.

## Definitie

**Robotics as a Service (RaaS)** biedt robotica-oplossingen via een abonnementsmodel waarbij bedrijven robots kunnen gebruiken zonder grote investeringen in hardware. Het pakket omvat robots, software, onderhoud en updates tegen een maandelijkse vergoeding.

## Kenmerken

- **Subscription model**: Monthly payments instead van large upfront investments
- **All-inclusive service**: Hardware, software, maintenance, upgrades included
- **Scalable deployment**: Easy addition or removal van robots based on demand
- **Professional support**: Expert technicians handling installation en maintenance
- **Continuous updates**: Regular software improvements en new capabilities
- **Performance monitoring**: Real-time analytics on robot utilization en productivity
- **Risk mitigation**: Service provider handles technical obsolescence risks
- **Flexible contracts**: Short to long-term agreements based on business needs
- **Training included**: Operator training en support as part van service package

## Toepassing

**Manufacturing applications:**
- **Welding operations**: Collaborative welding robots voor steel fabrication shops
- **Material handling**: Automated loading/unloading systems voor CNC machines
- **Quality inspection**: Vision-guided robots voor dimensional measurement
- **Assembly tasks**: Precise component assembly in automotive suppliers

**Small-medium enterprises:**
- **MKB metalworking**: Access to advanced robotics without capital investment
- **Seasonal production**: Robots deployed during peak production periods
- **Pilot projects**: Testing automation feasibility before full commitment
- **Skills shortage**: Robot assistance when skilled welders are unavailable

**Logistics en warehousing:**
- **Order fulfillment**: Automated picking en packing robots
- **Inventory management**: Autonomous mobile robots voor stock counting
- **Loading/unloading**: Heavy material handling in distribution centers
- **Sorting systems**: Automated package sorting voor shipping operations

**Service industries:**
- **Cleaning robots**: Autonomous floor cleaning in large facilities
- **Security patrols**: Mobile robots providing surveillance capabilities
- **Food preparation**: Automated cooking en food handling systems
- **Healthcare support**: Delivery robots in hospitals en care facilities

**Benefits realization:**
- **Reduced capital risk**: Pay-per-use model reduces financial exposure
- **Faster deployment**: Rapid implementation without procurement delays
- **Technology access**: Latest robotics technology available to smaller companies
- **Predictable costs**: Fixed monthly costs improving budget planning
