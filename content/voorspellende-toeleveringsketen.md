*Voorspellende toeleveringsketen (predictive supply chain)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en innovatie & toekomsttrends.

## Definitie

**Voorspellende toeleveringsketen (predictive supply chain)** is een geavanceerde supply chain management benadering die [[artificial-intelligence|AI]], [[machine-learning|machine learning]] en [[predictive-analytics|predictive analytics]] gebruikt om toekomstige vraag, leveringsproblemen en logistieke uitdagingen te voorspellen en proactief te addresseren.

## Kenmerken

- **Demand forecasting**: AI-powered vraagvoorspelling gebaseerd op historische data en trends
- **Supply risk prediction**: Early warning systems voor leverancier- en materiaalrisico's
- **Inventory optimization**: Automated stock level adjustments gebaseerd op voorspellingen
- **Real-time visibility**: End-to-end supply chain monitoring en tracking
- **Predictive maintenance**: Equipment failure prediction preventing supply disruptions
- **Dynamic routing**: AI-optimized logistics en transportation planning
- **Scenario modeling**: What-if analysis voor supply chain contingency planning
- **Automated decision making**: ML-driven supply chain optimization decisions
- **Exception management**: Automated alerts en responses to supply chain anomalies

## Toepassing

**Manufacturing supply chain:**
- **Raw material planning**: Steel, aluminum, chemical voorspelling voor production planning
- **Supplier risk management**: Leverancier financial health en delivery performance prediction
- **Production scheduling**: Demand-driven manufacturing planning optimization
- **Inventory balancing**: Work-in-process en finished goods inventory optimization

**Procurement optimization:**
- **Price forecasting**: Commodity price prediction voor strategic purchasing
- **Supplier selection**: Performance-based leverancier evaluation en ranking
- **Contract negotiation**: Data-driven pricing en delivery term optimization
- **Quality prediction**: Incoming material quality forecasting

**Logistics en distribution:**
- **Transportation optimization**: Route planning gebaseerd op traffic en weather predictions
- **Warehouse management**: Dynamic storage allocation en picking optimization
- **Delivery scheduling**: Customer delivery window optimization
- **Cross-docking**: Real-time material flow optimization

**Demand planning:**
- **Sales forecasting**: Multi-variate demand prediction models
- **Seasonality analysis**: Cyclical demand pattern recognition en planning
- **Customer behavior**: Purchase pattern analysis en prediction
- **Market trend analysis**: External factor impact on demand forecasting

**Risk management:**
- **Disruption prediction**: Natural disaster, geopolitical risk assessment
- **Supplier diversification**: Risk-based supplier portfolio optimization
- **Capacity planning**: Production capability forecasting en expansion planning
- **Financial risk**: Cash flow en working capital optimization
