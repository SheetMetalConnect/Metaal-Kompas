*Extended Reality (XR)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en digitale transformatie & industrie 4.0/5.0.

## Definitie

**Extended Reality (XR)** is een overkoepelende term voor alle immersieve technologieën die de grens tussen fysieke en digitale werelden vervagen, waaronder Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR) en Mixed Reality (MR).

## Kenmerken

- **Reality-virtuality continuum**: Spectrum from real environment to completely virtual
- **Immersive experiences**: Full sensory engagement through visual, audio, haptic feedback
- **Real-time rendering**: High-performance graphics voor smooth interactive experiences
- **Spatial tracking**: 6DOF (degrees of freedom) head en hand positioning
- **Cross-platform compatibility**: Unified development for VR, AR, MR devices
- **Natural interaction**: Gesture, voice, eye tracking voor intuitive control
- **Persistent content**: Digital assets anchored to physical or virtual locations
- **Multi-user support**: Shared experiences across distributed teams
- **Content creation tools**: Accessible development platforms voor non-programmers

## Toepassing

**Training en education:**
- **Safety procedures**: Virtual hazardous environment training zonder real risks
- **Equipment operation**: Hands-on practice with expensive machinery through VR
- **Assembly training**: Step-by-step guidance using AR overlays on real components
- **Skill certification**: Standardized assessment in controlled virtual environments

**Design en prototyping:**
- **3D modeling**: Immersive design review van mechanical components
- **Ergonomic testing**: Virtual workplace evaluation voor human factors
- **Client presentations**: Interactive product demonstrations in virtual showrooms
- **Collaborative design**: Multi-user design sessions across geographic locations

**Manufacturing support:**
- **Work instructions**: AR guidance overlaid on production equipment
- **Quality control**: XR-based inspection procedures met visual comparisons
- **Maintenance support**: Mixed reality diagnostic information on machinery
- **Process optimization**: Virtual simulation van production line changes

**Remote collaboration:**
- **Expert assistance**: Remote specialists providing guidance through AR
- **Global teamwork**: Virtual meetings in shared 3D workspaces
- **Knowledge transfer**: Immersive documentation van complex procedures
- **Field service**: On-site technicians receiving remote expert support

**Marketing en sales:**
- **Product demonstrations**: Virtual showcases van large industrial equipment
- **Customer training**: Interactive tutorials voor product usage
- **Trade shows**: Virtual booths en immersive product experiences
- **Configuration tools**: AR visualization van custom product options
