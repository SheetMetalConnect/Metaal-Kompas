*Autonome mobiele robots (AMR)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en digitale transformatie & industrie 4.0/5.0.

## Definitie

**Autonome mobiele robots (AMR)** zijn zelfrijdende robots die zich onafhankelijk door industriële omgevingen kunnen bewegen zonder externe geleiding. Ze gebruiken sensoren, camera's en AI voor navigatie en kunnen taken uitvoeren zoals transport, inventaris en inspectie.

## Kenmerken

- **Autonomous navigation**: Self-directed movement zonder physical infrastructure
- **SLAM technology**: Simultaneous Localization And Mapping voor environment understanding
- **Obstacle avoidance**: Dynamic path planning around people, equipment, obstacles
- **Fleet coordination**: Multi-robot coordination voor optimized workflows
- **Load handling**: Various payload capacities en attachment mechanisms
- **Safety systems**: Emergency stops, collision avoidance, human detection
- **Wireless connectivity**: Real-time communication with fleet management systems
- **Adaptive behavior**: Learning from environment changes en operational patterns
- **Modular design**: Interchangeable top modules voor different applications

## Toepassing

**Material transport:**
- **Raw material delivery**: Steel plates, bars transported to machining centers
- **Work-in-progress**: Moving parts between production stations
- **Finished goods**: Automated transport to quality inspection areas
- **Tool delivery**: Cutting tools, fixtures delivered to production lines

**Warehouse operations:**
- **Inventory management**: Autonomous stock counting en location tracking
- **Order picking**: Goods-to-person systems voor efficient fulfillment
- **Replenishment**: Automatic restocking van production line materials
- **Cross-docking**: Direct transfer between receiving en shipping areas

**Manufacturing support:**
- **Machine tending**: Automated loading/unloading van CNC machines
- **Quality inspection**: Mobile inspection stations voor large components
- **Maintenance support**: Tool en parts delivery to maintenance technicians
- **Waste removal**: Automated collection van scrap metal en packaging

**Specialized applications:**
- **Heavy payload**: High-capacity AMRs voor steel coils, large castings
- **Clean room**: Sterile environments requiring contamination control
- **Hazardous materials**: Safe transport van dangerous chemicals
- **Outdoor operations**: Weather-resistant AMRs voor shipyard, construction sites

**Fleet benefits:**
- **Productivity increase**: 24/7 operation zonder breaks or shift changes
- **Safety improvement**: Reduced manual handling injuries
- **Flexibility**: Easy reconfiguration voor changing production needs
- **ROI optimization**: Data-driven deployment en utilization analysis
