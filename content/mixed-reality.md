*Mixed Reality (MR)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en digitale transformatie & industrie 4.0/5.0.

## Definitie

**Mixed Reality (MR)** combineert elementen van Virtual Reality en Augmented Reality door digitale objecten te plaatsen in de fysieke wereld waar ze kunnen interacteren met echte objecten. Gebruikers kunnen digitale en fysieke elementen tegelijkertijd manipuleren.

## Kenmerken

- **Physical-digital fusion**: Seamless integration van virtual objects met real environment
- **Spatial mapping**: Real-time 3D scanning van physical surroundings
- **Object occlusion**: Digital objects correctly hidden behind physical objects
- **Real-time interaction**: Simultaneous manipulation van physical en virtual elements
- **Persistent holograms**: Digital objects remain anchored to physical locations
- **Hand tracking**: Natural gesture control voor object manipulation
- **Voice commands**: Speech recognition voor hands-free operation
- **Collaborative spaces**: Multiple users sharing same mixed reality environment
- **Environmental understanding**: AI recognition van surfaces, objects, spatial relationships

## Toepassing

**Assembly guidance:**
- **Step-by-step instructions**: 3D holographic assembly guides overlaid on workpiece
- **Component identification**: Digital labels identifying parts en tools
- **Quality checkpoints**: Virtual inspection points with pass/fail indicators
- **Work instruction overlay**: Procedures displayed contextually on equipment

**Maintenance applications:**
- **Equipment visualization**: See-through views van machine internals
- **Diagnostic overlays**: Real-time sensor data displayed on equipment
- **Remote expert assistance**: Shared MR sessions for troubleshooting support
- **Maintenance history**: Historical service records displayed on equipment

**Training scenarios:**
- **Hands-on learning**: Practice on virtual equipment in real workspace
- **Safety training**: Hazard visualization without actual risk
- **Process simulation**: Manufacturing processes overlaid on actual line
- **Skill assessment**: Performance evaluation in mixed reality environment

**Design en prototyping:**
- **In-situ design review**: Virtual prototypes placed in actual production environment
- **Ergonomic studies**: Human factors testing with virtual workplace elements
- **Layout planning**: Equipment placement visualization in real facilities
- **Collaborative design**: Multi-user design sessions in physical space

**Quality control:**
- **Measurement overlays**: Digital measuring tools aligned with physical parts
- **Defect highlighting**: Visual indicators overlaid on problematic areas
- **Inspection workflows**: Interactive quality check procedures
- **Documentation capture**: Photo/video documentation with spatial context
