*Pressure Equipment Directive (PED)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en lasnormen & certificering.

## Definitie

**Pressure Equipment Directive (PED)** is Europese richtlijn 2014/68/EU die de essential safety requirements stelt voor het ontwerp, fabricage en conformiteitsassessment van pressure equipment en assemblies. Het harmoniseert veiligheidsnormen binnen de EU en vereist CE-marking voor commercialisering van drukdragende apparatuur.

## Kenmerken

- **Scope definition**: Applies to pressure equipment with maximum allowable pressure > 0.5 bar
- **Category classification**: Risk-based categorization (I, II, III, IV) determining conformity procedures
- **Essential safety requirements**: Fundamental safety principles voor design, materials, manufacturing
- **Conformity assessment**: Prescribed procedures including third-party certification
- **CE marking**: Mandatory marking indicating compliance met directive requirements
- **Technical documentation**: Required design calculations, material certificates, test records
- **Notified bodies**: Authorized third-party organizations voor conformity assessment
- **Market surveillance**: Member state enforcement en compliance monitoring
- **Harmonized standards**: European standards providing presumption of conformity

## Toepassing

**Pressure vessel manufacturing:**
- **Storage tanks**: Compressed air, process gas, liquid storage vessels
- **Heat exchangers**: Shell-and-tube, plate exchangers voor industrial processes
- **Process reactors**: Chemical, pharmaceutical reaction vessels
- **Steam boilers**: Industrial steam generation equipment

**Piping systems:**
- **Process piping**: High-pressure industrial piping networks
- **Steam distribution**: Steam supply systems en condensate return
- **Compressed air**: Shop air distribution systems
- **Hydraulic systems**: High-pressure hydraulic equipment

**Welding requirements:**
- **[[lasrobot-programmering|Weld procedures]]**: Qualified welding procedure specifications (WPS)
- **Welder certification**: EN ISO 9606 welder qualification requirements
- **Weld inspection**: Non-destructive testing en visual inspection requirements
- **Material traceability**: Material certificates en test documentation

**Quality assurance:**
- **Quality management**: [[iso-9001-kwaliteitsmanagementsysteem|ISO 9001]] quality system requirements
- **Material testing**: Impact testing, tensile testing, chemical analysis
- **Pressure testing**: Hydrostatic en pneumatic pressure tests
- **Documentation**: Design calculations, manufacturing records, test certificates

**Conformity assessment:**
- **Module selection**: Appropriate conformity assessment procedures
- **Third-party certification**: Notified body involvement voor higher risk categories
- **Technical file**: Complete documentation package voor compliance demonstration
- **Declaration of conformity**: Manufacturer's declaration of regulatory compliance
