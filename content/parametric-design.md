*Parametric design (parametrisch ontwerp)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en innovatie & toekomsttrends.

## Definitie

**Parametric design (parametrisch ontwerp)** definieert ontwerpen door parameters en regels die automatisch aanpassingen doorvoeren wanneer input-waarden wijzigen. Het enables snelle iteraties en massa-customization door relationele geometrie en constraint-based modeling.

## Kenmerken

- **Rule-based modeling**: Design constraints en relationships defining geometry
- **Associative geometry**: Changes cascade through related design elements
- **Variable-driven design**: Parameters control dimensions, features en properties
- **History-based workflow**: Design intent preserved through feature history
- **Constraint solving**: Automatic geometry adjustment maintaining design relationships
- **Family tables**: Multiple variants generated from single parametric model
- **Design automation**: Scripts en templates enabling rapid design generation
- **What-if analysis**: Quick exploration van design alternatives
- **Intelligent regeneration**: Automatic model updates when parameters change

## Toepassing

**Product customization:**
- **Structural steel**: Beam sizes, connection details adapted to specific building requirements
- **Sheet metal parts**: Bracket dimensions, hole patterns customized voor different applications
- **Machined components**: Bearing housings, flanges sized voor specific shaft diameters
- **Weldments**: Frame structures scaled voor different load requirements

**Manufacturing applications:**
- **Tooling design**: Jigs en fixtures parametrically designed voor different part variants
- **Mold design**: Injection molds adapted voor different product sizes
- **Fixture generation**: Work holding devices automatically sized voor workpiece variations
- **Production line layout**: Conveyor systems en equipment placement optimized parametrically

**Engineering optimization:**
- **Stress analysis**: Parametric FEA models exploring design space optimization
- **Material selection**: Properties-driven design with automatic material substitution
- **Weight optimization**: Thickness, feature sizing optimized voor strength-to-weight ratios
- **Cost analysis**: Design-to-cost optimization through parametric cost modeling

**Automation benefits:**
- **Design standardization**: Consistent design rules across product families
- **Error reduction**: Constraint-based modeling preventing design mistakes
- **Time savings**: Rapid generation van design variants
- **Documentation**: Automatic drawing updates maintaining design synchronization
