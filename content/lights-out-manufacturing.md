title: Lights‑out manufacturing
---
*Lights‑out manufacturing* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en digitale transformatie & industrie 4.0/5.0.

## Definitie

**Lights-out manufacturing** refereert aan volledig geautomatiseerde productie die 24/7 kan opereren zonder menselijke aanwezigheid. Het systeem is zo autonoom dat letterlijk de lichten uit kunnen omdat er geen operators aanwezig hoeven te zijn.

## Kenmerken

- **Complete automation**: No human intervention required during production cycles
- **Autonomous operation**: Self-managing systems met error detection en recovery
- **24/7 operation**: Continuous production zonder breaks or shift changes
- **Predictive maintenance**: Automated maintenance scheduling preventing unplanned stops
- **Quality control**: Automated inspection systems ensuring consistent quality
- **Material handling**: Robotic loading/unloading van workpieces en raw materials
- **Process monitoring**: Real-time sensor data analysis voor anomaly detection
- **Remote supervision**: Minimal oversight from remote monitoring stations
- **Self-optimization**: Continuous process improvement through machine learning

## Toepassing

**CNC machining:**
- **Precision parts**: High-volume production van metal components met tight tolerances
- **Tool management**: Automatic tool changing en tool wear monitoring
- **Work holding**: Automated workpiece clamping en positioning systems
- **Part inspection**: In-process measurement en quality verification

**Metal production:**
- **Steel processing**: Continuous casting en rolling operations
- **Aluminum extrusion**: Profile production met consistent cross-sections
- **Forging operations**: Automated hot forging met precise temperature control
- **Heat treatment**: Controlled atmosphere furnaces met automated loading

**Assembly lines:**
- **Component assembly**: Robotic assembly van electronic devices
- **Automotive parts**: Engine component assembly in automotive plants
- **Appliance manufacturing**: White goods assembly met consistent quality
- **Electronics production**: PCB assembly en testing in high volumes

**Additive manufacturing:**
- **3D printing farms**: Multiple printers operating simultaneously
- **Metal powder bed fusion**: Layer-by-layer metal part production
- **Post-processing**: Automated support removal en finishing operations
- **Quality assurance**: Automated dimensional inspection van printed parts

**Benefits realization:**
- **Cost reduction**: Lower labor costs en improved efficiency
- **Quality consistency**: Reduced human error en consistent output
- **Production capacity**: Maximized machine utilization through 24/7 operation
- **Competitive advantage**: Faster delivery times en lower production costs
