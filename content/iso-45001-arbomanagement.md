*ISO 45001‑arbomanagement* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en kwaliteitsmanagement & software.

## Definitie

**ISO 45001-arbomanagement (Occupational Health & Safety Management)** is een internationale standaard voor occupational health en safety management systems die organisaties helpt om workplace injuries, ill health en fatalities te voorkomen door systematische risk management en safety culture development.

## Kenmerken

- **Risk-based approach**: Systematic hazard identification, risk assessment en control
- **Leadership commitment**: Top management involvement in safety culture development
- **Worker participation**: Employee consultation en participation in safety decisions
- **Legal compliance**: Regulatory requirements identification en compliance management
- **Continuous improvement**: PDCA cycle voor ongoing safety performance enhancement
- **Performance monitoring**: Safety KPIs, incident tracking, audit findings
- **Competence management**: Training, awareness, competency development programs
- **Emergency preparedness**: Emergency response planning, drills, crisis management
- **Communication**: Internal en external safety communication protocols

## Toepassing

**Manufacturing safety management:**
- **Machine safety**: [[safety-systems|Safety system]] integration, lockout/tagout procedures
- **Welding safety**: [[lasrobot-programmering|Welding operation]] safety protocols, ventilation requirements
- **Chemical safety**: Hazardous material handling, exposure monitoring, PPE requirements
- **Ergonomics**: Workplace design, manual handling, repetitive strain injury prevention

**Risk management implementation:**
- **Hazard identification**: Systematic workplace hazard assessment procedures
- **Risk control hierarchy**: Elimination, substitution, engineering controls, administrative controls
- **Job safety analysis**: Task-specific risk assessment en control measures
- **Incident investigation**: Root cause analysis, corrective actions, lessons learned

**Safety culture development:**
- **Leadership engagement**: Management commitment demonstration, safety leadership training
- **Employee involvement**: Safety committees, suggestion systems, behavior-based safety
- **Safety training**: Orientation, job-specific training, refresher training programs
- **Communication**: Safety meetings, toolbox talks, safety performance communication

**Performance monitoring:**
- **Safety metrics**: Leading en lagging indicators, injury rates, near-miss reporting
- **Audit programs**: Internal audits, management reviews, external assessments
- **Document control**: Safety procedures, work instructions, emergency procedures
- **Management review**: Regular safety performance evaluation en improvement planning

**Regulatory compliance:**
- **Legal requirements**: Occupational health en safety law compliance
- **Standards integration**: Integration with [[iso-9001-kwaliteitsmanagementsysteem|ISO 9001]], environmental management
- **Certification process**: Third-party certification, surveillance audits, recertification
- **Documentation**: Safety manual, procedures, records, compliance evidence
