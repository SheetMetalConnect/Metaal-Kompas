*Engineering Change Order (ECO)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en productstructuur & documentatie.

## Definitie

**Engineering Change Order (ECO)** is een gecontroleerd proces voor het formeel wijzigen van product ontwerp, documentatie, processen of specificaties. Het ECO systeem waarborgt dat alle wijzigingen systematisch worden beoordeeld, goedgekeurd en geïmplementeerd met volledige impact analyse en traceability.

## Kenmerken

- **Change request formalisatie**: Gestructureerde aanvraag met reden en impact assessment
- **Approval workflow**: Multi-disciplinaire review en goedkeuring proces
- **Impact analyse**: Complete assessment van technische, cost en schedule gevolgen
- **Effectiviteits planning**: When-effectief dates en implementation timeline
- **Document controle**: Automatic update van alle gerelateerde documentatie
- **[[traceerbaarheid-en-productidentificatie|Traceability]]**: Complete audit trail van change rationale en implementation
- **Cross-functional coordination**: Engineering, manufacturing, quality, sourcing alignment
- **Customer communication**: Formal notification waar contractueel vereist

## Toepassing

**Design wijzigingen:**
- **[[engineering-bom|eBOM]] updates**: Component additions, deletions, substitutions
- **CAD model changes**: Dimensional updates, feature modifications, interface changes
- **Material specifications**: Grade changes, surface treatments, heat treatment updates
- **Tolerance adjustments**: GD&T modifications, fit en clearance updates

**Manufacturing process changes:**
- **[[manufacturing-bom|mBOM]] modifications**: Production structure en assembly sequence updates
- **[[productierouting|Routing]] changes**: Work center modifications, operation additions/deletions
- **Tooling updates**: Fixture changes, cutting tool specifications, setup procedures
- **[[controleplan|Control plan]] revisions**: Quality control point modifications

**ECO proces flow:**
- **Change request**: Problem identification en proposed solution documentation
- **Preliminary assessment**: Engineering feasibility en initial impact estimate
- **Formal review**: Cross-functional team evaluation en approval/rejection
- **Implementation planning**: Timeline, resources, communication plan development
- **Execution**: Controlled rollout met [[first-article-inspection|FAI]] validation waar needed
- **Closure**: Effectiveness verification en lessons learned capture

**Metaalindustrie ECO drivers:**
- **Design optimization**: Weight reduction, cost improvements, performance enhancements
- **Manufacturability**: DFM improvements, tooling optimization, assembly simplification
- **Quality improvements**: [[corrective-and-preventive-action|CAPA]] driven design changes
- **Supplier changes**: Alternative sourcing, supplier capability changes
- **Regulatory compliance**: Standards updates, certification requirement changes
- **Cost reduction**: Material substitution, process improvements, design simplification

**Digital ECO management:**
- **[[product-lifecycle-management|PLM]] integration**: Automated workflow en document control
- **[[enterprise-resource-planning|ERP]] synchronization**: BOM updates, costing updates, MRP re-calculation
- **[[manufacturing-execution-system|MES]] updates**: Work instruction changes, quality plan updates
- **Supplier portals**: Automatic notification van affected suppliers
