*Long Range Wide Area Network (LoRaWAN)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en industriële communicatieprotocollen.

## Definitie

**LoRaWAN (Long Range Wide Area Network)** is een low-power, wide-area networking protocol ontworpen voor wireless IoT-apparaten. Het biedt lange afstand communicatie (tot 15km) met minimaal energieverbruik voor batterij-powered sensors en meters.

## Kenmerken

- **Long-range communication**: Up to 15km in rural areas, 2-5km in urban environments
- **Ultra-low power**: Battery life van 5-10 years voor sensor applications
- **Low data rates**: Optimized voor small data packets (0.3-50 kbps)
- **Star topology**: Sensors communicate directly with gateways
- **Unlicensed spectrum**: Uses ISM bands (868MHz in Europe, 915MHz in US)
- **Adaptive data rate**: Dynamic optimization van power consumption en range
- **Encryption security**: AES-128 encryption protecting data transmission
- **Scalable network**: Single gateway can handle thousands van end devices
- **Bi-directional**: Both uplink en downlink communication capabilities

## Toepassing

**Remote asset monitoring:**
- **Tank level monitoring**: Fuel, chemical, water tank levels in remote locations
- **Pipeline monitoring**: Pressure, flow monitoring over long distances
- **Equipment tracking**: Location en status van mobile equipment, containers
- **Environmental monitoring**: Air quality, water quality in industrial areas

**Utility applications:**
- **Smart metering**: Gas, water, electricity consumption monitoring
- **Grid monitoring**: Power quality, outage detection in distribution networks
- **Street lighting**: Remote control en monitoring van LED lighting systems
- **Waste management**: Fill-level sensors in containers, route optimization

**Agricultural/outdoor:**
- **Soil monitoring**: Moisture, pH, temperature sensors in large agricultural areas
- **Weather stations**: Distributed meteorological data collection
- **Livestock tracking**: Animal location en health monitoring
- **Irrigation control**: Remote valve control en soil moisture monitoring

**Industrial site management:**
- **Perimeter security**: Intrusion detection around large industrial facilities
- **Parking management**: Occupancy sensors in large parking areas
- **Air quality**: Pollution monitoring around industrial complexes
- **Noise monitoring**: Sound level measurement near residential areas

**Supply chain:**
- **Cold chain monitoring**: Temperature tracking during transportation
- **Cargo tracking**: Location en condition monitoring van shipping containers
- **Fleet management**: Vehicle location, fuel consumption monitoring
- **Logistics optimization**: Real-time tracking voor delivery optimization

**Network advantages:**
- **Low infrastructure cost**: Minimal gateway requirements voor large coverage
- **Easy deployment**: Simple sensor installation without complex networking
- **Scalable growth**: Easy addition van new sensors to existing network
- **Reliable coverage**: Good penetration through buildings en vegetation
