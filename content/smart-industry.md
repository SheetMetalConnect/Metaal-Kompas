*Smart Industry* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en digitale transformatie & industrie 4.0/5.0.

## Definitie

**Smart Industry** is de Nederlandse invulling van Industrie 4.0, gericht op digitale transformatie van de maakindustrie door technologieën zoals IoT, AI en robotica. Het streven is om de Nederlandse industrie concurrerender, duurzamer en flexibeler te maken.

## Kenmerken

- **Nederlandse aanpak**: Focus op MKB en Dutch industrial strengths
- **Public-private partnership**: Government, research institutes en companies collaboration
- **Digital connectivity**: Integration van IT/OT systems in manufacturing
- **Data-driven production**: Real-time analytics voor process optimization
- **Flexible manufacturing**: Adaptive production systems voor mass customization
- **Sustainable focus**: Environmental impact reduction through efficiency
- **Human-centered design**: Technology supporting rather than replacing workers
- **Innovation ecosystems**: Regional clusters van companies, knowledge institutes
- **Circular economy**: Resource efficiency en waste reduction priorities

## Toepassing

**Nederlandse metaalindustrie:**
- **Staalproductie**: Tata Steel digitalization initiatives met energy efficiency focus
- **Scheepsbouw**: Damen Shipyards digital twins voor vessel design optimization
- **Machinebouw**: ASML precision manufacturing with nanometer-level control
- **Automotive**: VDL Nedcar smart manufacturing voor electric vehicle production

**MKB implementaties:**
- **CNC machining**: Small metalworking shops implementing IoT monitoring
- **Laswerk bedrijven**: Adaptive welding systems voor quality improvement
- **Plaatwerk**: Sheet metal companies using digital work instructions
- **Maintenance services**: Predictive maintenance offerings using sensor data

**Regional initiatives:**
- **Brainport Eindhoven**: High-tech manufacturing ecosystem met global companies
- **Haven Rotterdam**: Port digitalization voor supply chain optimization
- **Chemie clusters**: Chemical industry Smart Industry implementations
- **Agri-food sector**: Food processing automation en sustainability

**Innovation projects:**
- **Field Labs**: Practical testing environments voor Smart Industry concepts
- **Pilot projects**: Small-scale implementations demonstrating ROI
- **Knowledge sharing**: Best practices distribution across industries
- **Skills development**: Training programs voor digital manufacturing

**Sustainability goals:**
- **Energy efficiency**: Production optimization voor reduced consumption
- **Waste reduction**: Circular economy principles in manufacturing
- **CO2 reduction**: Environmental impact minimization through digitalization
- **Resource optimization**: Material usage efficiency through data analytics
