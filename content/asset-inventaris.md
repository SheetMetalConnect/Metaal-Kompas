*Asset‑inventaris (asset inventory)* is een begrip binnen de industriële digitalisering en extra industrie & security topics.

## Definitie

**Asset-inventaris** is een systematische catalogus van alle fysieke en digitale assets binnen een organisatie, inclusief machines, software, netwerkcomponenten en kritieke infrastructuur. Het biedt visibility en controle voor onderhoud, security en compliance.

## Kenmerken

- **Complete visibility**: Volledige catalogus van alle organisatie assets
- **Asset categorization**: Classificatie naar type, kriticaliteit en eigenaar
- **Lifecycle tracking**: Registration vanaf aanschaf tot disposal
- **Real-time updates**: Live status van asset condition en location
- **Integration capabilities**: Koppeling met [[computerized-maintenance-management-system|CMMS]], [[enterprise-resource-planning|ERP]] systemen
- **Automated discovery**: Network scanning voor automatische asset detectie
- **Risk assessment**: Vulnerability en security risk profiling per asset
- **Compliance reporting**: Regulatory documentation en audit trails
- **Change management**: Tracking van asset modifications en updates

## Toepassing

**Manufacturing equipment:**
- **Machine inventory**: Complete catalogus van productie machines en specifications
- **Tool tracking**: Cutting tools, fixtures en precision instruments
- **Spare parts management**: Critical components en replacement parts inventory
- **Calibration tracking**: Measurement equipment calibration schedules

**IT/OT asset management:**
- **Network infrastructure**: Switches, routers, industrial gateways inventarisatie
- **Software licensing**: Application licenses en compliance tracking
- **Security devices**: Firewalls, cameras, access control systems
- **Mobile devices**: Tablets, scanners, mobile computers voor operations

**Metaalindustrie specifiek:**
- **Smelting equipment**: Ovens, furnaces, casting equipment registration
- **Material handling**: Cranes, conveyors, AGV systems tracking
- **Quality equipment**: Testing machines, spectrometers, hardness testers
- **Safety systems**: Emergency equipment, ventilation, monitoring devices

**Maintenance planning:**
- **[[predictive-maintenance|Preventive scheduling]]**: Maintenance calendar based on asset data
- **Warranty tracking**: Equipment warranty status en service contracts
- **Performance monitoring**: Asset efficiency en utilization metrics
- **Failure analysis**: Historical failure data voor reliability improvement

**Compliance en security:**
- **Regulatory compliance**: Asset documentation voor industry regulations
- **Security posture**: Vulnerability assessment en patch status per asset
- **Access control**: Asset access permissions en security classifications
- **Incident response**: Asset involvement in security incidents
